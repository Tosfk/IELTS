# 1. Understanding the Topic card
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tosfk/Image/main/wechat_2025-04-22_224844_480.png)

只有前后必须回答，三小问其实可以不说，或者自己调整。只要符合要求就行

比如问题是帮助你学习或工作的人，你讲你的妈妈，然后第二问是怎么认识她。  
？  
可以换成妈妈的性格，怎么照顾我的，工作是什么，学识很渊博所以可以帮助我  

最好不要说父母，可以说邻居

注意：
拿到练习卡时，一定要先看这四个部分。
- key words 先看关键词， a place
- modifiers 限定/修饰成分
- Tense 注意时态
- Focus of topic

# 2. Brainstorming Method 1

## 思考框架
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tosfk/Image/main/20250423101736630.png)

背景介绍 30s
扣题描述 60s
总结概括 30s

背景介绍包括但不限于
时间不够可以扣题描述只说两点
多说一些自己的总结感受，考官会更喜欢

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tosfk/Image/main/20250423101939229.png)
让步拉回能体现出更高级的语言能力

## 练习
```
Describe an exciting trip you once took.
You should say:
- where you went
- how you travelled there
- what you did during the trip
and explain how you felt about the trip.
```
练习-分析：
- 关键词：旅行：哪里，方式，过程
- 描述：激动
- 时态：一般过去时
- 扣题：
分析：
1. 背景介绍：复述题目
Ok, so I'm gonna talk about an exciting trip that I took. (重复题目，话题清晰；因为要快速回答，所以第一句话要求稳，重复题目最简单，不用同义替换)
- 带一个**事件**
- a few months ago
- end of summer classes
- needed a break
- with my boyfriend
- Chongqing

2. 扣题描述 save the best for last，描述+评价

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tosfk/Image/main/20250423105440246.png)

3. 总结概括
- 积极
  - special
  - relaxing
- 消极 (说个无关痛痒的东西)
  - tired
- 让步拉回
  - not a big deal
  - worth it

此时一段激情表演

## 5W1H
### Step1: Pick 3 new points that make sense

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tosfk/Image/main/20250423113250405.png)

介绍一个人是谁：
- where：where we meet？
- how： how we first meet？/ how they are？ / 性格
- when：我们现在什么时候会见面？
- what：外貌

### Step2：Turn them into your own questions
变成自己的问题
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tosfk/Image/main/20250423113413687.png)
不过这样说肯定不符合逻辑顺序，自己排序

### Step3：Rearrange questions logically
1. Who this person is
2. How you guys met
3. When you see them
4. What they look like

### Step4: Answer these questions, about 2 sentences each
**Who this person is:**
- This person is my best friend, Anne. People always say that we're like two peas in a pod.  
**How you guys met:**
- So we first met about 10 years ago, when Anne and her family movedin next door and my parents invited them over to our house for a welcome-to-the-neighborhood dinner.

> 习语：`two peas in a pod` 表示两个人关系特别好。best firend

拆解：从头说到位比较困难，把每个问题当作一个片段来练习，熟练了再合起来练习。

**When you see them:**  
- We used to see each other pretty much everyday, cause we went toschool together, but now it's only when we come back home fromcollege.

**What they look like:**
- And Anne, she is so beautiful, you know, she's got this jet-black hairthat's really silky, like in those shampoo commercials, and I'm awfullyjealous of her fair skin. l don't think she ever got a zit in her life.
此处像是精心准备过的内容，心善人也没，合适，串进来

### Step for Brainstorming
- Step1: Pick 3 new points that make sense for each question
- Step2: Turn them into your own questions
- Step3: Rearrange questions logically
- Step4: Answer all questions, about 2 sentences each.
- Step5: lf last Q is about feelings, choose 2, add reason or detail

第一个方法更好做，除非题刷了很多了有固定套路。这需要创造很多小问题，比较费脑。第一个方法不需要创作太多问题，只需要想三个大方向就够了。
